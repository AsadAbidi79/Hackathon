Join us in 1st Digital Health Pakistan Hackathon!

Pakistan’s 1st Digital Health Hackathon is a unique event to deconstruct and reconstruct innovative digital solutions that aim to preserve and strengthen people’s health in this advancing age of technology. Healthcare professionals, marketers, IT specialists & students and other domain experts come under one roof to collaborate on new landmark health innovation.

The healthcare industry is currently dealing with novel and unprecedented challenges. Digital Health Pakistan Hackathon 2023 Platform for Sustainable health Innovation

The Digital Health Pakistan Hackathon provides a chance to identify talent and open up new opportunities. The complexity of the clinical thought process and the variables that influence quality improvement need to be understood by non-clinicians and open new arenas of research and development. This is an all-nighter hackathon that will allow participants to collaborate and work effectively in teams to come up with solutions on topics from our 4 segments which will be disclosed at the event!
